geom2d.polyline¶

Polyline functions.

A polyline being a series of connected straight line segments.

geom2d.polyline.closest_point(polypath: Iterable[Line], p: P, prefer_normal: bool = False) → P | None¶

Get the closest point on a polyline to point p.

Parameters:

  • polypath – An iterable of line segments.

  • p – Reference point.

  • prefer_normal – Give preference to normal projection on line segment over closest endpoint.

geom2d.polyline.is_closed(polypath: Sequence[Sequence[Sequence[float]]]) → bool¶

True if polypath is a closed polygon.

Is polypath1 inside polypath2?

geom2d.polyline.length(polypath: Iterable[Sequence[Sequence[float]]]) → float¶

Total cumulative length of polypath.

geom2d.polyline.polyline_to_polypath(polyline: Iterable[Sequence[float]]) → Iterable[Line]¶

Convert a polyline to a polypath.

Parameters:

polyline – An iterable of vertices.

Returns:

An iterable of line segments.

geom2d.polyline.polypath_to_polyline(polypath: Iterable[Sequence[Sequence[float]]]) → Iterable[Sequence[float]]¶

Convert a polypath to a polyline.

Parameters:

polypath – An iterable of line segments.
